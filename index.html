Color Laser Shooter

Welcome to Color Laser Shooter

This is a game to fight stress!
You shoot the ball with required color, don't let them fall.

Things to do before playing this game

  • Plug in your earphones/headphones
  • Turn your volume to MAX
  • Shoot the ball, enjoy the sound, and feel relaxed
